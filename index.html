Writing

My writing has appeared in publications including Another Gaze: A Feminist Film Journal, Apollo, Artforum, the Guardian's Saturday magazine, the ICA Bulletin, Masterpiece, Outland, Paper Visual Art, and The Telegraph.


I have written texts for past and forthcoming Phaidon publications, including Vitamin T: Threads and Textiles in Contemporary Art (2019), Great Women Artists (2019), Great Women Painters (2022) and Vitamin C+: Collage in Contemporary Art (2023), and for gallery exhibition catalogues (see here and here).

Editing

I'm deputy editor of Outland, an online magazine about art, technology and NFTs. Past editorial projects include sub-editing two issues of Another Gaze, the exhibition catalogues for the 39th EVA International, and a couple of artist monographs (including for the Freud Museum in London). I was web editor of Apollo from 2017–21.

Conversations

I was the creator and host of the Apollo 40 Under 40 podcast for three series (in 2018, 2019 and 2021) as well as a three-part series on NFTs, which came out in May 2022. You can listen to all episodes here and here. I have also hosted live talks, online and in person, for London Art Fair, Aesthetica's Future Now Symposium (where I was on the art prize jury in 2021), and Hales Gallery.
