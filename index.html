Editing

I'm deputy editor of Outland, an online magazine about art and technology. Past editorial projects include commissioning for Critical Edge and sub-editing two issues of Another Gaze, the exhibition catalogues for the 39th EVA International, and a couple of artist monographs (including for the Freud Museum in London). I was web editor of Apollo from 2017–21.

Conversations

I was the creator and host of the Apollo 40 Under 40 podcast for three series (in 2018, 2019 and 2021) as well as a three-part series on NFTs, which came out in May 2022. You can listen to all episodes here and here. I have also hosted live talks, online and in person, for Unit London (watch that here), London Art Fair, Aesthetica's Future Now Symposium (where I was on the art prize jury in 2021), and Hales Gallery.

Curating

Once upon a time I studied museum curating at the Courtauld Institute of Art. I worked on exhibitions at the ICA, and organised a show of moving-image work at Hales New York.
