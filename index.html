Writing

You can read my many interviews, features and reviews for Apollo magazine here. My writing has also appeared in Another Gaze: A Feminist Film Journal, Outland, Paper Visual Art, and The Telegraph.

I have written texts for past and forthcoming Phaidon publications, including Vitamin T: Threads and Textiles in Contemporary Art (2019) and Great Women Artists (2019), and for exhibition catalogues (see here and here).
