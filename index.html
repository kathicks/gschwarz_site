Writing

You can read my many interviews, features and reviews for Apollo magazine here. My writing has also appeared in Another Gaze: A Feminist Film Journal, Artforum, the Guardian's Saturday magazine, the ICA Bulletin, Masterpiece, Outland, Paper Visual Art, and The Telegraph.


I have written texts for past and forthcoming Phaidon publications, including Vitamin T: Threads and Textiles in Contemporary Art (2019), Great Women Artists (2019) and Great Women Painters (2022), and for exhibition catalogues (see here and here).

Editing

I was web editor of Apollo from 2017–21. I sub-edited two issues of Another Gaze and the exhibition catalogues for the 39th EVA International, and have edited a couple of artist monographs, including for the Freud Museum in London.

Conversations

I was the creator and host of the Apollo 40 Under 40 podcast for three series (in 2018, 2019 and 2021) as well as a three-part series on NFTs, which came out in May 2022. You can listen to all episodes here and here. I have also hosted live talks, online and in person, for London Art Fair, Aesthetica's Future Now Symposium (where I was on the art prize jury in 2021), and Hales Gallery.
