I am a writer and editor specialising in contemporary art and culture. My essays, reporting and criticism have appeared in newspapers and magazines including Artforum, Artsy, Burlington Contemporary, Dirt, The Guardian, The New York Times, Paper Visual Art, The Telegraph, and The World of Interiors. I was previously the web editor of international art magazine Apollo and deputy editor of online digital art platform Outland. I've also hosted live talks and podcasts and contributed texts for books and exhibitions in London, New York, and beyond. In autumn 2025 I will be the writer in residence at Salzburger Kunstverein for its residency programme in partnership with Spike Art Magazine. I currently live in Cambridge in the UK.
